Technical SEO

Technical SEO Optimizations

Essential technical optimizations to improve your store's search engine visibility.

Use Google Search Console to submit your sitemap. This helps search engines crawl and index your site more efficiently.

Focus on LCP, FID, and CLS metrics. Use Shopify's native image optimization and avoid large apps.

Canonical tags help search engines understand the relationship between different versions of your content.

Automatic URL redirects help search engines understand the relationship between different versions of your content.

Robots.txt file helps search engines understand which pages you want them to crawl and index.

SSL certificate helps secure your site and improve its security.

Google Analytics 4 helps you understand how users interact with your site and how you can improve their experience.

Mobile-friendly design helps improve your site's performance and user experience on mobile devices.

404 error page helps users navigate to the correct page and improves your site's user experience.
